文字記錄

Your journal creates an opportunity to reconnect with yourself and explore difficult emotions. Many people keep a journal in their teenage years, either by choice or due to a school assignment, but journaling can have benefits for anyone at any stage of life.
Consider the following prompt:

Who do you trust most?
Why do you trust that person?

詞彙

opportunity: 機遇，時機，機會；可能性
reconnect: 重新接上，重新連上
explore: 探測；勘查；探索；研究
assignment: （尤指學業或工作方面的）任務，功課

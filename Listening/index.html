聽錄音，回答問題。

文字記錄

Randall:
Hey dad, what do you think of this restaurant? Pretty nice, don't you think?

Dad:
N’ah, it's okay.

Randall:
What do you mean, ‘it's okay’? The decor is great. Just look around you. So, shall we order?

Dad:
I don't know. Uh, maybe there's a better place.

Randall:
What do you mean, ‘a better place’? We've been up and down this street I don't know how many times. I think it's the best place we're going to find.

Dad:
I don't know. Well, I don't know.

Randall:
What are you talking about, you don't know. What - stop beating around the bush. Why don't you like this place?

Dad:
It's okay, but ….

Randall:
But what? Stop beating around the bush. Tell it to me straight.

Dad:
Well, I don't have any money. Uh yeah, I'm I'm out of cash.

Randall:
Well, I told you not to buy those outrageous shoes.

詞彙

beat around the bush: （說話）轉彎抹角，兜圈子
Don't beat around the bush - get to the point!
別再兜圈子——直接說出重點吧！

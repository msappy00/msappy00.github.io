詞彙

no telling: 不知道，無法知道
There is no telling what the future will hold for them.
不知道他們的未來會怎麼樣。
role: 地位；職責；作用；任務
What is his role in this project?
他在這個工程中負責甚麽？
roll with:
"roll with the punches"
to be able to deal with a series of difficult situations
渡過一道道難關；克服一連串困難

聽力練習

聽錄音，回答問題。

文字記錄

Edith: Somebody broke that.

Gru: Okay, okay. Clearly we need to set some rules.
Rule number one: You will not touch anything.

Margot: Uh-huh. What about the floor?

Gru: Yes, you may touch the floor.

Margot: What about the air?

Gru: Yes, you may touch the air.

Edith: What about this? [points gun at Gru]

Gru: Ahhh! Where did you get that?

Edith: Found it.

Gru: Okay, rule number two: you will not bother me while I’m working.
Rule number three: You will not cry or whine or laugh or giggle
or sneeze or burp or fart. So no, no no annoying sounds, alright?

Agnes: Does this count as annoying? [makes sound]

Gru: Very!

詞彙

rule: 規則；法則；規章；條例
You must follow/obey/observe the rules.
你一定要遵守規則。
floor: （房間的）地板，地面
The floor was partly covered with a dirty old rug.
部分地板上鋪著骯髒的舊地毯。
air: 空氣
I went outside to get some fresh air.
我到室外呼吸一下新鮮空氣。

文字記錄

Teacher:
Hello, can you both talk about child labor?

Novalee:
Sure, I've heard that child labor is a big problem in some parts of the world.

Phoenix:
Yes, it is. Children are often forced to work long hours in dangerous conditions instead of going to school.

Novalee:
That's terrible. What can be done to stop it?

Phoenix:
Governments and companies can take action to prevent child labor. They can enforce laws that protect children and ensure that businesses don't hire children under the legal working age.

Novalee:
What about consumers? Can they do anything to help?

Phoenix:
Absolutely. Consumers can make sure that the products they buy are made by companies that don't use child labor. They can also support organizations that work to end child labor.

Teacher:
That's right. Everyone can play a role in ending child labor. It's important to remember that every child deserves the chance to go to school and live a happy, healthy life.

詞彙

child labor: 童工
dangerous conditions: 危險情況
enforce laws: 執法
legal working age: 法定工作年齡
consumer: 消費者
product: 產品
organization: 組織

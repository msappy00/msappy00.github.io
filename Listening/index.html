The Blue House

Bea:
Dad, I have to finish my blue poster today.
It's due tomorrow.

Dad:
You can look for blue pictures in these magazines, Bea.
My library books are due tomorrow too.

[Bea finds a blue house and cuts it out.
Bea glues the blue house onto her poster.]

Dad:
Bea, did you get the blue house from this book?

Bea:
You said I could cut out the blue pictures, right?

Dad:
True, and you didn't know that this is a library book.
It's okay. I've always wanted to buy this book anyway.

詞彙

poster: 招貼，海報；佈告
The children put up posters on the classroom walls.
孩子們在教室的牆上張貼了海報。
magazine: 雜誌，期刊
She has written articles for several women's magazines.
她為幾家女性雜誌撰稿。
anyway: 不管怎樣；無論如何；仍然
Of course I don't mind taking you home - I'm going that way anyway.
我當然可以把你送回家——反正我也是順路。

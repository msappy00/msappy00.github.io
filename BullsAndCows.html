How to Play:

You have eight guesses to guess a four digit number.
If a digit is correct and in the correct position,
that is a 'bull' and is shown as a 'B' in the results.
If a digit is correct, but not in the correct position,
that is a 'cow' and is shown as a 'C' in the results.
